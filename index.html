More Context

Instead of being purely academic, TVET programs prepare you for specific careers—like mechanics, IT, healthcare, construction, or business. Our school offers web-development and marketing programs.

This program is built on international standards, drawing ideas from places like Australia and the Philippines, but adapted to the Ethiopian context. It offers high school level training (Grades 11–12 and post-10th grade), with pathways through TVET Certificate Levels I to IV.

The curriculum is designed by training institutions and is largely decentralized, so it can vary from place to place.
